Order Types

Three ways to enter
every trade.

From risk-first bracket orders to sophisticated averaging — every execution style covered.

Bracket Order
Risk-first execution

Define risk %, entry price, static stop-loss and multiple profit targets before entry. Core Terminal sizes the position automatically based on your account balance.

Custom Order
Full manual control

Set precise order parameters yourself — size, limit price, post-only, reduce-only. Everything a professional trader needs, with complete flexibility.

AVG-In Order
Scale into positions

Automatically average into a position across multiple price levels. Define your ladder and let the terminal handle the execution across all levels.

Platform Features

Everything a pro needs.

Every feature built around speed, clarity, and reducing time between idea and execution.

📊
Multi-Chart View

Up to 4 live charts side by side — each with independent symbol, timeframe and drawing tools. Toggle between Single and Multi layout in one click.

⚡
Chart Trading

Market Buy and Sell directly from the chart. "Use brackets" checkbox auto-applies your TP/SL template to market orders without touching the sidebar.

🎯
Multiple Profit Targets

Add unlimited profit targets per trade with individual ratio and quantity settings. 100% runner support keeps a portion open beyond your last target.

⚙️
Risk Management

Max risk cap slider (0.5%–20% of balance), static SL %, confirmation modals configurable separately for Table and Chart contexts, and sound alerts.

✏️
Drawing Tools

Built-in chart drawing: Horizontal Lines, Rectangles, and Fibonacci Retracement. Mark key levels without leaving the terminal.

📐
Leverage Control

Type any leverage value supported by the exchange (up to 125x), or use the quick-select slider. Choose Cross or Isolated margin mode and apply with one button.

📋
Positions & Orders Panel

Full real-time view with Time, Symbol, Type, Side, Price, Amount, Filled, Reduce Only, Trigger Conditions, TP/SL, and bulk cancel all.

🧪
Testnet Support

Each API connection includes a testnet toggle. Test strategies with paper money before going live — no separate account needed.

🔒
Encrypted API Storage

Keys encrypted locally with your password. Core Terminal never sees raw keys. Stolen session cookies can't be reused on other devices or IPs.
